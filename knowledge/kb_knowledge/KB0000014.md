## Description

<p><span style="font-size: 18pt;"><b>Upgrade your Mac Operating System</b></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">These are the specs required for upgrading to newer versions o OS X:</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><b>Requirements for OS X 10.9 (Mavericks)</b></span></p>
<p>OS X 10.9 works with these Macintosh computers:</p>
<ul><li>iMac (Mid 2007 or newer), MacBook (Late 2008 Aluminum, or Early 2009 or newer), MacBook Pro (Mid/Late 2007 or newer), Xserve (Early 2009), MacBook Air (Late 2008 or newer), Mac mini (Early 2009 or newer), Mac Pro (Early 2008 or newer)</li><li>2GB of memory</li><li>OS X v10.6.8 or later</li><li>8GB of available space</li></ul>
<hr />
<p><b><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Requirements for OS X 10.8 (Mountain Lion)</span></b><span></span></p>
<p>OS X 10.8 works with these Macintosh computers:</p>
<ul><li>iMac (Mid 2007 or newer), MacBook (Late 2008 Aluminum, or Early 2009 or newer), MacBook Pro (Mid/Late 2007 or newer), Xserve (Early 2009), MacBook Air (Late 2008 or newer), Mac mini (Early 2009 or newer), Mac Pro (Early 2008 or newer)</li><li>2GB of memory</li><li>OS X v10.6.8 or later already installed</li><li>8GB of available space</li></ul>
<hr />
<p><span><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Requirements for Mac OS X 10.7 (Lion)</span></strong></span><br /><span>Mac OS X 10.7 works with these Macintosh computers:</span></p>
<ul><li>Mac computer with an Intel Core 2 Duo, Core i3, Core i5, Core i7, or Xeon processor</li><li>2GB of memory</li><li>OS X v10.6.6 or later (v10.6.8 recommended) already installed</li><li>7GB of available space</li></ul>
<hr />
<p><span><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Requirements for Mac OS X 10.6 (Snow Leopard)</span></strong></span><br /><span>Mac OS X 10.6 works with these Macintosh computers:</span></p>
<ul><li>Mac computer with an Intel processor</li><li>1GB of memory</li><li>5GB of available disk space</li><li>DVD drive for installation</li></ul>

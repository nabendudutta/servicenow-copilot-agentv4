## Description

<p><strong>Symptoms</strong></p>
<p>You can&#39;t log in to your laptop at the login screen</p>
<p><strong>Cause</strong></p>
<p>In the last 30 minutes you might have entered your password wrong at least 5 times *</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>2 possible resolutions :</strong></p>
<p>1. Wait 30 Minutes for the account to <strong>automatically unlock </strong><strong>itself. </strong></p>
<p>2. Contact IT Support for immediate assistance (by telephone or desk visit)</p>

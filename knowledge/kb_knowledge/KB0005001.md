## Description

<p>Knowledge Base Article outlining Workstation Endpoint Security Hardening Standard.</p>
<h1>Mac</h1>
<h3>Casper Management Framework</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Agent installed at first image (Casper Imaging used to deploy new machines)</li><li>LaunchAgent installed at first image to verify Casper enrollment, and re-enroll as needed</li><li>Regular policy check-in set to every hour</li><li>Full inventory update run daily</li></ul>
<h3>Antivirus</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Sophos installed on all Macs at first image (set to auto update)</li><li>Smart group in Casper to look for Macs without Sophos or needing repair
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Product version</li><li>Primary update server</li><li>ParentAddress router configuration</li></ul>
</li><li>Policy in Casper to re-install Sophos as needed</li></ul>
<h3>Encryption</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>FileVault 2 installed on all Macs at first image</li><li>Smart group in Casper to look for Macs without encryption
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Boot drive encryption check</li><li>Provides encryption status (not encrypted, encrypting, pending encryption, decrypted, encrypted)</li></ul>
</li><li>Policy in Casper to force encryptions as needed</li></ul>
<h3>Password Configuration</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>All Macs are bound to AD</li><li>Passwords and PINs used to authenticate to any network, compute, storage, or cloud service
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Passwords must be at least 12 characters long</li><li>Passwords must consist of at least three of the following: upper case letters, lower case letters, numbers and special characters</li><li>PINs must be at least 4 numbers long</li><li>Passwords must be changed every 90 days</li></ul>
</li></ul>
<h3>Session Timeout/Lock</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Auto-login not enabled</li><li>Login screen restricted by FileVault 2</li><li>ScreenSaver &amp; wake from sleep force lock</li><li>ScreenSaver set to engage at 15 minutes</li><li>Local accounts only - no guest access</li></ul>
<h1>Windows</h1>
<h3>LanDesk Management Framework</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Agent installed at first image</li><li>Inventory policy check-in set to once a day and on IP change</li><li>Distribution and Patch policy set to check-in When user logs in (once per login) When IP changes and every 2 hours there after.</li></ul>
<h3>Antivirus</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Sophos installed on all PCs at first image (set to auto update)</li><li>LADESK software distribution policy set for deployment to any system detected not running Sophos</li></ul>
<h3>Encryption</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>BitLocker (MBAM 2.5) Configured at time of deployment</li></ul>
<h3>Password Configuration</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>All PCs are bound to AD</li><li>Passwords and PINs used to authenticate to any network, compute, storage, or cloud service
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Passwords must be at least 12 characters long</li><li>Passwords must consist of at least three of the following: upper case letters, lower case letters, numbers and special characters</li><li>PINs must be at least 4 numbers long</li><li>Passwords must be changed every 90 days</li></ul>
</li></ul>
<h3>Session Timeout/Lock</h3>
<ul style="list-style-position: inside;"><li>Controlled by Group Policy</li><li>Screensaver set to engage at 15 minutes</li></ul>
<p> </p>
